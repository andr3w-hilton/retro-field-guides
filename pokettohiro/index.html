POKETTOHIRO

GAME BOY COLOR · 2024
01 EARLY GAME · WATERMELON CHAIN PRE-DUNGEON ▼
Before you can access most of the world, you need to complete a short but non-obvious trading chain. Do this first — it unlocks the submarine and opens up the ocean floor.
🍉Find the cave shop on the path leading to the forest. Buy a Watermelon — this starts the chain.
👴Take the Watermelon to Akane's grandfather in the village. He trades it for a Snorkel.
🤿Give the Snorkel to Mizuki. In return, she grants you access to her Submarine — your route to the ocean floor.
💬Talk to every NPC in the village thoroughly before leaving — several trigger progression flags that are easy to miss. The game doesn't signal this well.
💰Check Mr. Adventure's house early — there is a breakable wall inside leading to a room with unlimited money. Worth finding before you need to buy anything.
💎Magic Crystal fragments are hidden in flowers and rocks throughout the world. Collect them as you explore — gathering all crystals unlocks a secret ending and direct dungeon access.
One crystal is hidden at the top of the village fountain — easy to miss on the way through.
02 FOREST OF ILLUSION DUNGEON 1 ▼
🌲The forest has repeating sections designed to disorient. The exit is not immediately obvious.
🌺Red flowers indicate the correct path. When you see them, follow their direction. Ignoring them and exploring freely will loop you back.
⚔You enter as the Hero — agile sword combat. Get comfortable with the attack timing here as it carries through the whole game.
UNLOCKS ARCHER — ranged precision attacks
03 OCEAN DUNGEON DUNGEON 2 ▼
Requires the Submarine from the Watermelon chain. Make sure that's done before heading here.
🚤Take Mizuki's Submarine to reach the ocean floor. Navigate to the Ocean dungeon entrance.
🏹The Archer (unlocked from the Forest dungeon) is useful here — ranged attacks help with underwater enemy placements. Swap to them if you're struggling.
🗝Collect the Abyss Key found on the ocean floor — it's needed to progress further into the world.
UNLOCKS NINJA — enhanced jumping, elevated area access
04 DESERT TEMPLE DUNGEON 3 ▼
🏜The Ninja's enhanced jump is useful for navigating elevated sections on the way to the desert. Use them to reach areas the Hero cannot.
🔍Pay close attention to NPC dialogue here — the desert has event flag requirements that are easy to miss. If you seem stuck, you've likely skipped a conversation.
💎Several crystal fragments are hidden in this area — check destructible rocks and flowers as you pass through.
UNLOCKS MAGE — reveals hidden objects, doors, and crystals
The Mage is one of the most useful characters for exploration — swap to them whenever you suspect something is hidden nearby.
05 VOLCANO · MATCHBOX CHAIN DUNGEON 4 ▼
A second trading chain begins here. It ultimately unlocks access to the Ice Palace — complete it as you go rather than backtracking later.
🌋Navigate to the Volcano via the mountain areas. Some mountain paths require the Bird Man — but Bird Man is unlocked by completing this dungeon. On first pass, use the Hero or Ninja to reach the entrance.
🔥Inside the volcano, find the Matchbox. This is the start of the second trading chain.
🎁Exchange the Matchbox for a Gift Voucher.
🎻Trade the Gift Voucher at the ocean shop for a Violin.
🧣Exchange the Violin for a Scarf — required to access the Ice Palace.
UNLOCKS BIRD MAN — flight, full mountain area access
06 SKY DUNGEON DUNGEON 5 ▼
🦅Use the Bird Man to access the mountain areas fully and reach the Sky Dungeon entrance — flight opens up sections that were previously inaccessible.
☁Revisit earlier mountain areas with the Bird Man — there are crystals and items in elevated spots you couldn't reach before.
🔍Use the Mage to reveal any hidden doors or objects before leaving each area — the sky region has several.
UNLOCKS FAIRY — access to narrow passages and hidden routes
07 ICE PALACE DUNGEON 6 ▼
Requires the Scarf from the Matchbox trading chain. If you don't have it, complete the Volcano chain before entering.
❄Equip the Scarf to gain entry. Without it the ice area is inaccessible regardless of what characters you have.
🧚The Fairy can access narrow passages in the Ice Palace that other characters cannot fit through — swap to her when you hit a dead end.
💎Use the Mage to reveal hidden objects before progressing through each room — the Ice Palace has several concealed items and doors.
🗡By this stage you should have all six characters available. Experiment with switching — each character's ability solves different obstacles throughout the dungeon.
★ FINAL BOSS TOWER · BLACK KNIGHT FINAL ▼
🏰The Final Boss Tower opens after the Ice Palace. All six characters are available — bring your best understanding of each one's ability.
⚔Face the Black Knight — the villain who shattered the Magic Crystal. Combat is not the hardest part of this game; reaching the boss with all flags met is the challenge.
💎If you have gathered all crystal fragments, you will unlock the secret ending after defeating the Black Knight — see Secrets section below.
The game auto-warps you back to town after some dungeons complete. If this happens unexpectedly, it's normal — re-navigate from the village.
▸ CHARACTERS ▼
Characters are unlocked by completing dungeons. Swap freely — most puzzles and blockages have a specific character solution.
HEROStarting character. Agile sword combat. Well-rounded — reliable default when no specific ability is needed.
ARCHERRanged precision attacks. Good for enemies at a distance or in awkward positions. Unlocked: Forest dungeon.
NINJAEnhanced jump height. Use to reach elevated platforms and areas the Hero cannot. Unlocked: Ocean dungeon.
MAGEReveals hidden objects, doors, and crystal fragments. Use in any area where you suspect something is concealed. Unlocked: Desert temple.
BIRD MANFull flight. Required for mountain areas. Go back and re-explore elevated zones after unlocking. Unlocked: Volcano dungeon.
FAIRYAccesses narrow passages other characters can't fit through. Key for Ice Palace. Unlocked: Sky dungeon.
▸ TRADING CHAINS ▼
Both chains are required for progression. Complete them as you reach each area — backtracking to finish them later wastes time.
CHAIN 1 — SUBMARINE ACCESS
Buy Watermelon→ Trade to Akane's Grandfather→ Snorkel→ Give to Mizuki→ ✦ Submarine Access
CHAIN 2 — ICE PALACE ACCESS
Matchbox (Volcano)→ Gift Voucher→ Violin (ocean shop)→ ✦ Scarf
▸ SECRETS ▼
💰Infinite money: Inside Mr. Adventure's house, there is a breakable wall. Smash it to find a room that yields unlimited money. Find this early.
💧Water Bottle: Also found in Mr. Adventure's house. Pick it up — it's used somewhere in progression.
⛲Hidden crystal: Top of the village fountain. Easy to run past. Grab it on your first visit to the village.
🔮Fortune teller and secret shop exist somewhere in the world. Keep an eye out — NPCs may hint at their locations.
🏆Secret ending: Collect all crystal fragments, defeat the Black Knight, then return to the village and fully traverse the Cave of Despair. Face a giant ghost and meet Mr. Adventure for the true ending.
▸ ESSENTIAL TIPS ▼
💬Talk to everyone, twice: Progression gates in this game are often hidden NPC flags. If you're stuck and haven't exhausted dialogue, that's the first thing to try.
🗺No map: There is no in-game map. Mentally note landmarks as you explore. The red flowers in the Forest of Illusion are the most explicit navigation aid in the game.
🔍Use the Mage constantly: Any time you're in a new area and suspect something is hidden, swap to the Mage and use their reveal ability. Crystals and doors are frequently concealed.
💾Save often: Save points are plentiful. Use them. The event flag system can leave you stuck in unexpected ways if you haven't saved recently.
🔄Auto-warp: Completing some dungeons will automatically warp you back to the village. This is intentional — re-navigate from there.
💎Crystal hunting: Gather fragments as you go through each area rather than hunting them all at the end. The Mage makes this much easier once unlocked.
